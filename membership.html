FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the leads verified before delivery?+
Yes. Every buyer enquiry is manually verified by our team before it is delivered to you. We check that the buyer is genuine, their contact information is accurate, and that their requirements match your product category.
How long does it take to receive my first lead?+
Your Relationship Manager will contact you within 24 hours of activation to set up your profile. You can typically expect your first verified lead within 7–14 days of profile completion, depending on your product category and lead availability.
Can I upgrade my plan later?+
Yes. You can upgrade your membership plan at any time. The difference in plan price will be calculated on a pro-rata basis for the remaining membership duration. Contact your Relationship Manager to upgrade.
What happens if I do not receive all my leads?+
If the full number of leads promised in your plan is not delivered within the membership year, unused leads are carried forward or addressed in the next renewal cycle. Your RM will proactively manage this for you.
Is GST registration really included?+
Yes. GST registration assistance is included in all membership plans. We guide you through the full application process, document requirements, and status follow-up at no additional cost.
How do I pay for my membership?+
Payment can be made via bank transfer, UPI, or online payment gateway. Once payment is confirmed, your account is activated and your RM contacts you within 24 hours. Contact us at Buygenixsolutions@gmail.com or WhatsApp for payment details.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is available Monday–Saturday, 10am–6pm IST. Talk to us before you choose your plan — no pressure, just guidance.
