Who We Are

An Independent Platform Built For Indian Exporters

Our Story

BuyGenix Solutions is an independent Indian B2B export-import platform founded to bridge the gap between Indian manufacturers and exporters and verified international buyers.

We provide verified buyer leads, dedicated relationship management, digital marketing support, and full export compliance assistance to help Indian businesses compete on the global stage.

We are not a marketplace or an aggregator. We are a growth partner — a team of export consultants who work directly with each member to understand their business and deliver measurable results.

Six principles that shape how we work, how we serve our members, and how we grow together.

Every lead manually checked. We never send unqualified or cold leads. Our standard is verified, relevant, and ready-to-buy.

🤝

Human Partnership

Global Ambition
